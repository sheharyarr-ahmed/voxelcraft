What this is — and what it isn't

VoxelCraft applies pre-trained, license-verified public LoRAs and composes them with ControlNet. It is not a training pipeline — no model was fine-tuned by the author, and there is no GPU training here. The training notebook in the repo is a documented methodology reference, not an executed run.
